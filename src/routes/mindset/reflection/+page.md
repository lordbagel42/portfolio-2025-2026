# Reflection

The yurt trip was odd, but amazing. I spent most of it just reflecting on myself and the people around me, to summarize it. I absolutely loved it; it's the most peaceful I've felt in a long time. Being able to truly just walk away from my problems was amazing, and the pride I felt knowing I had pulled the sled just under 5 miles was wonderful. I'm disappointed that I still haven't done anything with all the footage I recorded, and need to do something with it soon.
