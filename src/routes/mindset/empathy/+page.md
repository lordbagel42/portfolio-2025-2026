# Empathy

I spend most of my time in the Foundry helping others. A great example is helping Owen build his potato cannon, where I gave a lot of continued feedback as he built it on how to do so (tool usage, for example). I just wanted to see him succeed with it.

In Human vs Machine, I spent a good chunk of time working with and coaching other students on how to use different devices and tools in the Foundry. For example, I crash coursed a group of students through Onshape and the ideas behind parametric CAD to make a router baseplate.
