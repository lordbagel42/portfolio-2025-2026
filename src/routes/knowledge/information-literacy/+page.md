# Information Literacy

Trial & error along with asking other people is the way I learn almost everything. High school is a good place to throw things at the wall and see what happens without hurting something. Welding, for example—it’s not like the trailer we made has to handle a ton of forces, I can teach people on it without fear of ruining things (too much). Asking others is really the best way to learn, though. The best people don’t have blogs saying how to weld, but they will answer your random questions.
